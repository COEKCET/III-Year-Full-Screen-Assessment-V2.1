Exam Details
Structure & marking scheme
▼
  • 50 MCQ questions — aptitude + department-specific topics.
  • Total duration is 90 minutes. Timer begins right after entering the exam.
  • Questions are randomised and shuffled uniquely for each candidate.
  • No negative marking. Attempt all questions.
  • Each question carries 1 mark. Maximum score: 50.
🗺️
Navigation & Answering
How to move through questions
▼
  • Save & Next — saves your answer and moves to the next question.
  • Mark for Review — flags the question (yellow) to revisit later.
  • Question Palette — Green = answered · Yellow = review · Purple = answered + review · Grey = unanswered.
  • You can change your answer at any time before final submission.
  • Use Review All to see a summary of answers before submitting.

Submission Rules
Final submission guidelines
▼
  • System will auto-submit when the 90-minute timer expires.
  • You can manually submit at any time using the "Submit" button on the last question.
  • After submission, re-login is not possible. Your session is permanently locked.
  • Results are saved securely on the server immediately upon submission.
  • If network fails at submission, results are saved locally and retried automatically.
